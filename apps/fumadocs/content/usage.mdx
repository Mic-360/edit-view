## Steps

1. Open the web app.
2. Upload one image (JPG/PNG/WEBP up to 5MB).
3. Enter a short prompt (max 500 characters), e.g., "Add a sunset background".
4. Click Submit.
5. Wait for processing; the edited image will appear with a download button.

If an error occurs, it will show below the result section and in a toast.
